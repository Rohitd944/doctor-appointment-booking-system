Welcome to Kullolli Hospital

Kullolli Hospital is a trusted multispeciality hospital in Sangli & Miraj. We are committed to delivering world-class healthcare with a focus on compassion and excellence.

Our facility offers 24x7 emergency services, an advanced ICU & Cardiac ICU, modern diagnostics, and expert doctors across a wide range of specialties. Your health is our priority.

Our Centres of Excellence

Cardiology & ICU

Advanced cardiac care and 24/7 critical support in our state-of-the-art ICU.

Orthopedics

Comprehensive care for bone and joint health, from trauma to joint replacement.

Diagnostics

Modern pathology and radiology (X-Ray, USG) for precise and timely diagnosis.

Pediatrics

Specialized, compassionate care for infants, children, and adolescents.

24/7 Emergency Care

Our emergency department is always open and ready to provide immediate medical attention.

Ophthalmology

Advanced eye care services, from routine check-ups to complex eye surgeries.
